  I began playing cello at age 38, after many years of studying guitar and other instruments. I had taken music theory in college and had a rather eclectic musical background, but had never really achieved much in the way of instrumental performance or repertoire. I had decided however, that I wanted to become a competent performing musician.
My first cello teacher gave me a lot of material to learn, but I had decided to move from Seattle to the San Francisco Bay Area, and was looking for a new teacher, perhaps one that could work with my idiosyncratic ideas about music and my background. I found Irina through an Internet search, and went to her studio for a lesson just a few days after arriving in the Bay Area. I was amazed and intrigued from the very first lesson about her pedagogical style and her care and sensitivity in approaching music and the cello. I was especially enthusiastic about the way she began to build my trust and motivation to learn to really play music beautifully, both sonically and technically .
I had extensive experience with private music lessons, especially on guitar, but I had never experienced such thorough instruction on the movement and visualization techniques that make a musician really shine on their instrument, such as how Irina delivered her instruction.
From the beginning , we worked on music specific to my level of ability on the instrument, and she gently but persistently pushed me to improve my technical ability and understanding of the music. In two years of weekly lessons, I went from barely being able to hold the bow to performing Bach suites. Irina also helped me to choose the first, second and third cellos that I've purchased as I advanced in my ability.
She helped me to set goals and to progressively work towards more and more challenging music.
One conductor friend of mine even told me that I am very lucky to have such a teacher, because the Russian string instrument pedagogy is truly the best in the world. I certainly agree with him.
Beyond that, Irina truly and wholeheartedly cares about me as a person, and displays heartfelt empathy and care in guiding me along my path as a musician and a person.

Irina is helping me to fulfill my commitment to myself to become the musician I always wanted to be.

      -Trey Donovan
